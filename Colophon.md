## Technical requeriments ##

* Hardware
    - a computer with a stable operating system. In this project we use this hardware (not mandatory):
        - Macbook 15"
        - Macbook Air 11" (_not eligible for data-table-grid cleaning_)
        - Macbook 13"
        - 21" Dell monitor
